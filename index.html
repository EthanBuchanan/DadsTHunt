What's Going On!?!

Santa was ambitious this year and planned to give gifts to not just all humans, but all species


The elves jumped to the task, but have just made a mistake. Two elves carrying comically large piles of tags crashed into each other spilling the tags in a jumbled mess. But these aren't just any old present tags, these are tags used to taxinomically map out all the species they are going visit on Christmas.


The tags need to put back in their right order. The Family tags are in the right order, but the genus tags need to be put in the right family, and the right species tags need to be put in the right genus. After this the genus tags need to be alphabetically sorted within each family based on their scientific name. Then the same needs to happen with the species tags within each genus.
